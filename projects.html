Shooting Game Software

 

(course project for Fundamentals of Computing)

I led a group of two and designed a shooting game software with beautiful UI. It was developed with C++.

Main functions include:

  • Sensitive detection of whether mouse clicks hit the moving objects, simulating gun shooting scenarios

  • Self-adaptive speed and direction adjustment of objects according to difficulty level and user performance

  • Storage and retrieval of history game records of different users
