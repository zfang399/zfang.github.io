Shooting Game Software

 

(course project for CSE 20311 - Fundamentals of Computing)

I led a group of two and designed a shooting game software with beautiful UI. It was developed with C++.

Main functions include:

  • Sensitive detection of whether mouse clicks hit the moving objects, simulating gun shooting scenarios

  • Self-adaptive speed and direction adjustment of objects according to difficulty level and user performance

  • Storage and retrieval of history game records of different users

Malware Detection for Windows Machines

 

(course project for CSE 40647 - Data Science)

I led a group of three and implemented machine learning algorithms for detecting malwares in Windows machiens. It was written in Python. Please check the poster on the left.

Main work include:

  • Experimented with two classification methods:

    • XGBoost

    • feedforward neural network

A Case Study for US Flight Delays and Cancellations

   

(course project for ITAO 30210 - Data Analysis with Python)

I led a group of two and conducted a case study for US flight delays and cancellations from a data scientist's point of view.

Main work include:

  • Came up with the following observations:

    • Flight delays happen more often later in the day compared to earlier in the day.

    • Flight delays happen more often from May to August than the rest of the year.

  • Studied the location distribution of the states with highest average flight delays, as well as those with lowest average flight delays.
