Research

My research interests lie in the intersection of data mining and machine learning, as well as their applications in low-resource languages and publication networks in academia. Generally speaking, I am interested in analysing and making use of the information that a relatively specific group of people have curated. Specific topics include but are not limited to:

  • Text Mining

  • Information Extraction

  • Conputational Behavior Modeling

  • Graph Mining
